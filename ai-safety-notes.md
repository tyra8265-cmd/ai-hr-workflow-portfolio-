# AI Safety Notes for HR Workflows

AI can be useful in HR and workforce operations, but it should not replace human judgment.

## AI Can Help With

- Organizing information
- Summarizing messages
- Drafting first versions
- Creating checklists
- Flagging risks
- Preparing follow-up notes
- Finding patterns
- Improving visibility

## AI Should Not Make Final Decisions About

- Hiring
- Rejection
- Termination
- Promotion
- Compensation
- Accommodations
- Employee relations outcomes
- Legal or compliance decisions
- Disciplinary action

## Human Review Should Be Required When

- The topic involves pay
- The topic involves medical or disability information
- The topic involves accommodations
- The topic involves legal risk
- The topic involves discrimination or harassment
- The topic involves employee complaints
- The topic involves termination
- The topic involves final hiring decisions

## Default Safety Rules

- Draft only
- Human approval before sending
- No invented facts
- No final HR decisions
- Sensitive issues get escalated
- Keep an audit trail
- Use least-access permissions
- Do not expose confidential employee or candidate information

## Point of View

AI should help HR teams move with more clarity, consistency, and follow-up.

It should not replace responsibility, care, or judgment.
